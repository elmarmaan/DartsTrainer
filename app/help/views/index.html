Purpose

This app will help you take your dart skills to the next level! It covers all the games you need to become a pro.

Games

501/301/101

With these games you can test your skills against an opponent of your desired strength. You can give the opponent a three dart average which you think you can beat.
Improves: scoring and doubles

Statistics

The most important thing in becoming a dart legend, is to keep track of your progress. You can see several statistics for each game type and each game type has his own level.

Levels per game type

501/301/101
Beginner: three dart average below 40
Mediate: three dart average from 40 to 60
Semi pro: three dart average from 60 to 85
Pro: three dart average from 85 to 100
Expert: three dart average 100 and above
